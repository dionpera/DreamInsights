Cottage - Simplicity and Comfort

Dreaming of a cottage often symbolizes simplicity, comfort, and a desire for a peaceful life. It may suggest that you are seeking tranquility and a more laid-back lifestyle, or it could represent a return to nature and a desire for grounding. A cottage in a dream may signify your need for emotional safety and comfort, or it may reflect your yearning for a more authentic and simple life.
