Dream Insight - Lion

"The dream is the small hidden door in the unconscious that leads to the whole liberation of the self." - Carl Jung

Lion - Strength and Courage

The lion symbolizes strength, courage, and leadership. Dreaming of a lion can represent your inner strength or your need to assert yourself in a situation. It might also indicate a powerful influence in your life, or your own desire to become more confident.

If the lion is aggressive, it may indicate unresolved anger or a need to control certain situations. If the lion is calm, it may indicate a harmonious state of confidence.
