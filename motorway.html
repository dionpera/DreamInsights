Motorway - Journey and Direction

Dreaming of a motorway often symbolizes the direction and speed of your life's journey. Motorways are fast-paced roads that take you quickly from one place to another, suggesting that you may be moving quickly toward a goal or life stage. It could also indicate the need to focus on the road ahead and keep your eyes on the larger objective.

If you're stuck in traffic or feel lost on the motorway, it might represent obstacles or delays in your life. Alternatively, a sense of freedom on the motorway could indicate that you're on the right path and feel empowered in your journey.
