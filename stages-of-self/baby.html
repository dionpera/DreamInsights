Baby - New Beginnings and Vulnerability

Dreaming about being a baby often symbolizes a fresh start, innocence, or a return to a state of purity. It may represent new opportunities, untapped potential, or a need for nurturing and care.

When dreaming about being a baby, it might also indicate feelings of vulnerability or a desire to be cared for. It can suggest a need to reconnect with a simpler, more innocent time in life.
