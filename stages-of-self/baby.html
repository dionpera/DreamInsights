Understanding the Baby Stage in Dreams

Dreaming about being a baby typically represents vulnerability, innocence, or a new beginning. It might indicate a need for protection or a desire to return to a simpler time of life.

When dreaming of being a baby, it may symbolize feelings of helplessness, dependence, or a need to nurture your emotional well-being. It can also represent the early stages of a new project or a fresh start in your life.
