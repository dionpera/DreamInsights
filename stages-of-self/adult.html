Adult - Responsibility and Independence

Dreaming about being an adult often reflects themes of maturity, responsibility, and personal growth. It may indicate the need to take control of your life, make important decisions, or assert your independence.

Such dreams might also highlight the pressures or challenges of adult life, suggesting a desire to find balance and reconnect with simpler times.
