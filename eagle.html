Dream Insight - Eagle

"The dream is the small hidden door in the unconscious that leads to the whole liberation of the self." - Carl Jung

Eagle - Higher Perspective

The eagle is a symbol of vision, perspective, and freedom. Dreaming of an eagle may suggest that you need to rise above the challenges in your life and see things from a higher perspective. It may indicate a desire to break free from constraints or limitations in your life.

If the eagle is flying high, it may suggest you are about to gain clarity on a situation, or that you should trust your intuition and instincts to guide you.
