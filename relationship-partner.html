Partner - Love and Conflict

Dreaming of your partner can reflect your desires, emotional intimacy, and issues within the relationship. Positive dreams about your partner often symbolize love, trust, and understanding. If your partner appears loving and caring in the dream, it may indicate the strength of the relationship in your waking life.

On the other hand, negative or troubled dreams about your partner may highlight underlying relationship issues. These could be related to feelings of neglect, jealousy, insecurity, or unresolved conflicts that need attention. Such dreams can offer insight into the emotional state of the relationship.
